IS-1034: Introduction to International Studies and Political Science

Description: Introduces students to the fields of International Studies and Political Science and their respective subfields. Focuses on inquiry, problem-solving, integration of ideas and experiences with a focus on International Studies and Political Science. Familiarizes students with the basic principles of the research and writing process.
