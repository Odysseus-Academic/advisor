HUM-1604: Introduction to Humanities and the Arts

Description: Explores the written, visual, and performing arts of selected periods and cultures, setting them in the context of their times. Study of these periods linked with overarching questions of cultural encounters, interactions, and negotiations. Introduces principles of each art form as well as the means of appreciation. Students taught methods in researching, writing, and presenting on these art forms.

Pathways: 6A Critique & Practice in Arts, 11 Intercultural&Global Aware.
